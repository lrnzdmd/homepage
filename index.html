About me...

Chirp chirp! I’m Larry (LeBirdie Terchirpiter Mc) Bird, a world-class chirper and proud holder of three Most Valuable Parrot awards. I love hanging out with my fluffy dog friend, munching on millet and sunflower seeds, and smashing watermelons. My elephant statue ride was a letdown, but hey, life’s still chirpy! When I’m not napping in a cozy hand, I’m busy being the chirpiest bird around. Chirp chirp!
